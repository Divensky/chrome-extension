Welcome to Reading Time extension!

Your newly installed extension will appear in the extensions menu (Puzzle) at the right top of your browser. Click on the puzzle piece, select your extension and press a pin button next to it. This will help quickly access your extension.

You can set the options for this extension on the Options page.

In case you have any trouble with this beta version, please don't hesitate to drop us a line through the Options page.

Enjoy this extension!
